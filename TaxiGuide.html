Gabès is a city in the south-east of Tunisia. The largest city in southern Tunisia after Sfax, Gabes has the distinction of being both an oasis and a seaport. This port function dates from the industrial development of the 1970s. Previously, Gabès was a harbor reputed to be difficult and dangerous.
